Reports deprecated section naming conventions in Robot Framework files.

This inspection detects the use of deprecated section headers like `*** Test Case ***` (singular) instead of the recommended `*** Test Cases ***` (plural) format. It helps maintain consistency with current Robot Framework best practices and ensures compatibility with newer versions.

The inspection will highlight deprecated section names such as:

  • *** Test Case *** → should be *** Test Cases ***
  • *** Keyword *** → should be *** Keywords ***
  • *** Setting *** → should be *** Settings ***
  • *** Variable *** → should be *** Variables ***

Example of deprecated usage: 

*** Setting ***
Library    Collections

*** Variable ***
${NAME}    Example

*** Test Case ***
My Test
    Log    Hello World

*** Keyword ***
My Keyword
    Log    Custom keyword

This inspection helps modernize Robot Framework test files by identifying outdated section naming conventions.
