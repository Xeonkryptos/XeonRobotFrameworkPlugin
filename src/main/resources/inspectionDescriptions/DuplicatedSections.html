Reports duplicate Robot Framework section headers by detecting multiple occurrences of the same section type in a single file.

This inspection highlights every repeated section (e.g. *** Settings ***, *** Variables ***, *** Test Cases ***, *** Tasks ***, *** Keywords ***) because Robot Framework expects each section at most once. Keeping only one instance per section improves clarity, prevents unexpected parser behavior, and avoids silently ignored content.

Why it matters: When a section is duplicated, Robot Framework will only process one occurrence; later duplicates can mislead maintainers and hide intended configuration or tests.

Typical fix: Merge the contents of all duplicates into a single section and remove the extras.

Example of a problem:

*** Settings ***
Library    Collections

*** Settings ***
Library    OperatingSystem

After fix:

*** Settings ***
Library    Collections
Library    OperatingSystem

In inspection settings you can disable or change severity if needed.
